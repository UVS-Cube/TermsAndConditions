Privacy Policy

MyBeat Cure – Clinical Management Platform

📅 Effective Date: July 7, 2026
1

Introduction

UVS Cube Infotech Private Limited (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the MyBeat Cure application. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal information processed within our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, disclosed, and protected when healthcare practitioners, admins, nurses, and receptionists utilize MyBeat Cure.

2

Information We Collect

To provide role-based healthcare administrative and clinical management services, MyBeat Cure processes the following categories of information:

Account Information

Any user with a valid mobile number can register and log in. We collect authentication details including mobile number, full name, email address, date of birth, and gender. Clinical and administrative access is then determined by the role assigned by a facility administrator.

Facility Data

We collect information regarding healthcare facilities (locations) created or linked by users, including facility names, addresses, and administrative permissions.

Patient and Clinical Data

Depending on the authorized user's role assigned by a facility admin, the application processes patient lists, clinical SOAP notes, appointments, assignment tasks, and discharge summaries.

3

How We Use the Information

MyBeat Cure utilizes the collected data strictly to operate, maintain, and improve the application features, including:

Identity Verification

Verifying identity and securing accounts via mobile OTP authentication.

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Enforcing RBAC to ensure that only authorized personnel (e.g., Doctors, Nurses, Receptionists) can access specific clinical or administrative modules.

Clinical Workflow Facilitation

Facilitating patient scheduling, task assignments, and medical documentation workflows within linked facilities.

4

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal or medical data to third parties. Data is only accessible within the application to users who have been granted explicit, role-based permission by their respective facility administrators.

We may disclose information only if required to do so by applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

5

Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal, facility, and clinical management data only for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to fulfill the operational healthcare services outlined in this policy. Because MyBeat Cure handles medical administration data, certain information—such as practitioner records, audit logs, and patient interaction histories—may be retained for extended periods to comply with applicable local statutory, legal, or medical health data retention requirements. Once data is no longer required under these obligations, it is securely deleted or permanently anonymized.

6

Data Deletion Process and User Rights

Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal information and account data at any time. You can submit a formal data deletion request through either of the following clear methods:

Online Contact Form

Submit your request electronically via our designated support portal at https://uvscube.com/contact/.

Email Request

Contact our support team directly by emailing support@uvscube.com from your registered email address with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Upon receiving a verified request, we will initiate the deletion of your account credentials and personal profiling data within 30 days, subject to any legally mandated medical data retention constraints mentioned in Section 5.

7

Security of Information

We implement stringent, industry-standard security measures — including biometric authentication, PIN-based login, OTP verification, and role-based access control — designed to safeguard personal and healthcare data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, because MyBeat Cure functions within facility environments, the overall security also depends on users maintaining confidential login credentials and facility administrators managing permissions responsibly.

8

Your Rights and Choices

Users can update their personal profiles, professional experience, and facility associations directly within the application settings. For account deletion or data-related inquiries, please contact our support team using the details provided in Section 9 below.

Age Restriction: MyBeat Cure does not impose age restrictions on account registration. Access to clinical and administrative data within a facility is strictly controlled through role-based permissions granted by authorised facility administrators.
